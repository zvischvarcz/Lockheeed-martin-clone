Our partnership is centered on securing UK's national interests while strengthening the country from within.

Partnering for Security & Prosperity

Lockheed Martin UK is the UK-based arm of Lockheed Martin Corporation and has operated in the country for more than 70 years.

From mail sorting technology to helping build the UK's first commercial spaceport, our innovations and partnerships help solve some of the world's most complex challenges, contributing to the UK's defence, security and prosperity.

21st Century Security Solutions Across All Domains

We partner with the Ministry of Defence on a variety of crucial defence and security programmes.

Cyber

We are delivering offensive and defensive cyber and electronic warfare capabilities to help our defence and intelligence customers protect their platforms, systems, networks and data.

Land

On land, our systems provide deterrence and, when necessary, promptly deliver the kinetic and non-kinetic effects needed to execute your mission.

Air

In the air, we are synchronising legacy and next-generation platforms to serve as an edge nodes of a network-centric operational architecture. The F -35 is the most advanced node in that network.

Space

Space is the ultimate high ground. With decades of experience delivering global communications, weather forecasting, space exploration and national security, we're ready to support UK Space industry growth.

Sea

We are bringing disruptive capabilities to the sea and undersea domains that will help our Navy customers execute distributed offensive and defensive operations in contested environments.
